VerifyAI FMCG Packaging & Label Compliance Standards
Document Code: FMCG-STD-2026-V1
Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION & SCOPE
This standard covers the mandatory packaging checks for food, beverages, and cosmetic items sold within the retail market.

2. MANDATORY LABEL DETAILS
Every FMCG retail item packaging label must clearly feature:
- Ingredient Declaration: A complete list of all ingredients sorted by weight in descending order.
- Allergen Warnings: Major allergens (e.g. nuts, dairy, soy, gluten) must be highlighted in bold or separately declared (e.g., "Contains: Peanuts").
- Batch Number and Expiration Date: Clear batch identifier numbers (e.g. B-XXXXX) and expiration dates (e.g., "EXP: DD/MM/YYYY" or "Best Before: MM/YYYY") must be visible.
- Compliance Badges: Visual compliance stamps (such as FDA, FSSAI, CE, ISO seals) must match verified graphics.

3. INSPECTION AND AUDITS
- Barcode Verification: Product barcodes must match GS1 prefix directories for product authenticity verification.
- Packaging Alterations: Damaged label printing or faded barcodes must be flagged for manual packaging review.
