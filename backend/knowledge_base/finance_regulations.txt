VerifyAI Finance Evidence Validation Regulations
Document Code: FIN-REG-2026-V3
Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION & SCOPE
This document outlines standard verification parameters for proof of income, bank account standings, and personal tax declaration checks.

2. INCOME STATEMENT COMPLIANCE
To support financial capacity claims, applicants must provide credit logs matching their declarations.
- Salary Matching: Deposited credit line amounts (net salary credits) must match salary slips or tax forms within a 5% margin of tolerance.
- Holder Alignment: The account owner name printed on bank statement headers must exactly match the applicant profile name (first and last name alignment).
- Official Stamp: Bank statement pages must display the official bank seal, stamp, or digital audit signature of the issuing financial institution.

3. RED FLAGS & TAMPERING DETECTION
- Font Discrepancies: Statements containing multiple font sizes, styles, or unexpected margin shifts indicate structural alteration. These are categorized as "Needs Review" or "Insufficient Evidence" depending on risk.
- Balance Continuity: Transaction opening and closing balances must match chronologically across consecutive statements. Broken balance chains indicate missing logs.

4. REQUIRED EXPOSURE
Applications lacking 3 consecutive months of bank statements or tax declarations are automatically statused as "Insufficient Evidence".
