VerifyAI Entertainment Ticket & Digital Rights Validation Guidelines
Document Code: ENT-GUIDE-2026-V2
Last Updated: April 05, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION & SCOPE
Provides checks for ticketing services, entry ticket validations, and copyright ownership documents.

2. TICKET SCAN COMPLIANCE
For digital tickets (concerts, movies, sporting events) to be validated:
- Barcode/QR Code: A high-resolution QR code or 1D barcode must be visible without overlay distortions.
- Transaction ID: The registration serial code (e.g. TIX-XXXXX-ENT) must match active reservation queues.
- Date and Time: The event date must correspond to the booking period. Expired tickets are rejected.
- Holder Identification: Ticket recipient names must match the customer profiles.

3. COPYRIGHT & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
- License Contracts: Copyright agreements must display active signatures of both the license holder and the publishing agency.
- Digital Seals: Registered contracts must feature cryptographic registry stamps.
