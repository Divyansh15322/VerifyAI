1. INTRODUCTION & SCOPE
This document outlines standard verification parameters for parts dimensional audits, material certifications, and assembly stamps.

2. QUALITY CERTIFICATION DETAILS
For a batch inspection report or material testing sheet to be certified:
- Material Grade: The testing sheet must state the verified material grade (e.g. Steel Grade 316, Aluminum 6061-T6).
- Dimensional Tolerance Check: Measurements must fall strictly within drawing specification margins. (e.g. standard margins are 25.00mm ± 0.05mm). Out of spec parts are rejected.
- Inspection Stamp: Reports must display the quality supervisor stamp and sign-off date. Missing stamps default to "Needs Review".

3. COMPLIANCE STANDARDS
- ISO 9001: Quality certificates must reference ISO 9001 compliance standards.
- Batch Identifiers: Part serial numbers must align with shipping cargo indexes.
